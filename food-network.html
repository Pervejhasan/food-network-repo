Thai-Soup

Thai-Soup is a good to way get needed water-soluble vitamins and minerals. Extremely soluble in water are Vitamin C and thiamine; only slightly soluble are riboflavin and niacin, which are found in green vegetables.Regularly consuming soup has been linked to a lower body weight.
